# Earnings Research

## Purpose

Research earnings-focused intelligence for upcoming reports, historical
surprises, guidance changes, call transcripts, and post-earnings drift.

## Candidate Technologies

- Earnings calendar APIs
- Transcript providers
- Earnings surprise datasets
- LLM transcript summarization
- Guidance-change extraction
- Event-study backtesting

## Evaluation Criteria

- Calendar accuracy
- Transcript and estimate availability
- Surprise prediction usefulness
- Guidance extraction precision
- Historical event-test performance
- Cost and licensing
- Integration difficulty with Atlas evidence scoring

## Integration Status

Research only. Atlas does not currently ingest earnings calendars or
transcripts.
