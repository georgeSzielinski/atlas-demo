# Institutional Research Reports

Atlas Institutional Research Reports are deterministic investment reports built
from existing Atlas engines and saved Atlas records.

## Sections

Reports include executive summary, recommendation, probability distribution,
confidence, knowledge, stability, executive review, investment committee, bull
case, bear case, catalyst timeline, SEC highlights, fundamentals, technicals,
forecast, news, portfolio impact, historical analogs, case studies, risk,
expected return, scenario analysis, research memory, and appendix.

## Outputs

- Markdown is generated now.
- PDF output is a future renderer placeholder.
- HTML output is a future renderer placeholder.

## Policy

Reports do not use an LLM. They do not guess missing data. Missing inputs are
reported as unavailable. Reports do not change BUY/HOLD/AVOID, provider
selection, thresholds, broker behavior, or execution.
